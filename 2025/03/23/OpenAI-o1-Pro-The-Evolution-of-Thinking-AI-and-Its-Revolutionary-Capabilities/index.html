Differences between GPT Series and o1 Series

It‚Äôs also important to understand the key differences between the GPT Series and o1 Series models:

GPT Series (e.g. GPT-4o): Multi-modal AI can process text, images, and audio simultaneously, and is excellent for real-time subtitle generation and image-based description automation. It is fast and supports a wide range of input and output formats.

O1 Series: Inference-driven models with the ability to think more deeply about complex problems. They are relatively slow but highly accurate for critical tasks, and are often used for coding, data analysis, and complex problem solving.
